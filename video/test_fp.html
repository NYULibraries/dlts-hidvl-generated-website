Video type:

Caveat: live streams are not yet officially supported. You should always test a live setup with your own streams.

Disclaimer: The live stream might be down sometimes.

You need to serve a HLS (html5) and an RTMP (Flash) stream.

With live streams it is mandatory to configure live: true or to add the is-live state class.

Also do not try to preload the video - which does not make sense with a live stream anyway. Either use a splash setup and/or add preload="none" as attribute to the video tag.

WebKiT browsers reset the Flash engine on fullscreen toggle, therefore you will notice that in Chrome there's a slight delay and the timer is reset, because the Flash engine must reconnect.

Note: This demo uses the 5.4.3 version of flowplayer.swf to work around a regression in the Flash engine regarding live streams.

Streams are picked in this order:

  1. HLS stream in the HTML5 engine in browsers which can play HLS
  2. RTMP stream in the Flash engine

You should play with different values for the preload video attribute depending on your streams.
